<div class="flddfn">
<h3>Definition</h3>
<p>This qualified generic field specifies:</p>
<table border="0" cellpadding="5" width="100%" class="qualifierdefinitiontab">
<tbody><tr valign="top">
<td width="13%"><p>BENE</p></td>
<td width="20%"><p>Beneficial Ownership Indicator</p></td>
<td width="67%"><p>Specifies whether there is change of beneficial ownership.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>BLOC</p></td>
<td><p>Block Trade Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the settlement instruction is a block parent or child.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>CASY</p></td>
<td><p>Cash Settlement System Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies what cash settlement system is instructed to be used.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>CCPT</p></td>
<td><p>CCP Eligibility Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the settlement transaction is CCP (Central Counterparty) eligible.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>COLA</p></td>
<td><p>Exposure Type Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the underlying business area/type of trade causing the collateral movement.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>COLE</p></td>
<td><p>Collateral Pool Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether securities should be included in the pool of securities eligible for collateral purposes.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>LEOG</p></td>
<td><p>Letter of Guarantee Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether physical settlement may be executed using a letter of guarantee or if the physical certificates should be used.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>MACL</p></td>
<td><p>Market Side Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether an instruction is for a market side or a client side transaction.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>NETT</p></td>
<td><p>Netting Eligibility Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the settlement transaction is eligible for netting.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>PAYM</p></td>
<td><p>Payment Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the instruction is free or against payment.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>REDE</p></td>
<td><p>Receive/Deliver Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the transaction is a receipt or delivery of financial instruments.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>REGT</p></td>
<td><p>Registration Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether registration should occur upon receipt.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>REPT</p></td>
<td><p>Repurchase Type Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the type of repurchase transaction.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>REST</p></td>
<td><p>Restrictions Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Restrictions governing the transfer of ownership of security.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>RTGS</p></td>
<td><p>Securities Real-Time Gross Settlement Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the settlement transaction is to be settled through an RTGS or a non RTGS system.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>SETR</p></td>
<td><p>Type of Settlement Transaction Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies underlying information regarding the type of settlement transaction.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>SETS</p></td>
<td><p>Settlement System/Method Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies whether the settlement instruction is to be settled through the default or the alternate settlement system.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>STAM</p></td>
<td><p>Stamp Duty Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the stamp duty type or exemption reason applicable to the settlement instruction.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>STCO</p></td>
<td><p>Settlement Transaction Condition Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the conditions under which the order/trade was to be settled.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>TCPI</p></td>
<td><p>Tax Capacity Party Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the tax role capacity of the instructing party.</p></td>
</tr>
<tr valign="top">
<td><p>TRCA</p></td>
<td><p>Party Capacity Indicator</p></td>
<td><p>Specifies the role of the party in the transaction.</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody></table>


</div>
